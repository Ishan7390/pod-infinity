About Pod Infinity

Pod Infinity is a group of enthusiastic video and audio editors that have experience of doing paid projects for multiple clients and have now assembled to help other people get started with podcasting.

Ishan Sharma took the initiative to help other people get started with podcasting after six months of starting his own podcast, BITS Cast. BITS Cast has over 60 episodes with over 17k listens on all platforms.

With Pod Infinity, you'll learn the best pratices while starting a podcast, and how to excel in it with some tips and tricks. In the mentorship plan, you'll learn how to edit, publish and market your podcast and in the production plan, you can simply focus on recording a great podcast episode with your guest and leave the rest(editing, publishing, marketing, etc.) to us!
